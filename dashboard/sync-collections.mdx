## Sync Colelction


1\. Navigate to [https://manage.uselooop.com/stores](https://manage.uselooop.com/stores)


2\. Choose the store you want to sync with

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-1.png" />
</Frame>

3\. Click "Collections"

<Frame>
<img src="/images/cs-1.png" />
</Frame>

5\. Click "Sync" button next to the collection you want to sync

<Frame>
<img src="/images/cs-2.png" />
</Frame>
This will add the selected collection to the sync queue. Once the sync is complete, you will see the colelction in your Webflow CMS.
