## Sync Single Product

1\. Navigate to [https://manage.uselooop.com/stores](https://manage.uselooop.com/stores)


2\. Choose the store you want to sync with

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-1.png" />
</Frame>

3\. Click "Products & Variants"

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-2.png" />
</Frame>


4\. Click "Sync" button next to the product you want to sync

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-3.png" />
</Frame>

This will add the selected product & its variants to the sync queue. Once the sync is complete, you will see the product in your Webflow CMS.

## Sync All Products

1\. Navigate to [https://manage.uselooop.com/stores](https://manage.uselooop.com/stores)


2\. Choose the store you want to sync with

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-1.png" />
</Frame>

3\. Click "Products & Variants"

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-2.png" />
</Frame>


4\. Click "Sync" button on the top right corner

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-4.png" />
</Frame>

This will add all the products & their variants to the sync queue. Once the sync is complete, you will see the product in your Webflow CMS.
