## Sync Policy

1\. Navigate to [https://manage.uselooop.com/stores](https://manage.uselooop.com/stores)


2\. Choose the store you want to sync with

<Frame>
<img src="/images/pvs-1.png" />
</Frame>


3\. Click "Shopify Policies"

<Frame>
<img src="/images/ps-1.png" />
</Frame>

4\. Click "Sync"

<Frame>
<img src="/images/ps-2.png" />
</Frame>

This will add the selected policy to the sync queue. Once the sync is complete, you will see the policy in your Webflow CMS.
