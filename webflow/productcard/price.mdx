title: 'Price & Comapre Price'
description: 'These elements are used to display the price of a product and compare it with the original price based on selected variants, country and subscription.'
---

## Setup

Add this attribute to any text blovk inside the product card to show the price.
```html
looop=price
```

Add this attribute to any text blovk inside the product card to show the compare price.
```html
looop=compareprice
```
